# Target region      : data/kras_target_region.txt

===========================================================================
1) Mutant-specific forward primer  (KRAS_G12D_Forward)
===========================================================================
Sequence : 5'- ACTTGTGGTAGTTGGAGCTGA -3'
Length   : 21 bp
GC%      : 47.6%
Tm       : 58.2 °C  (Primer3-derived)
Notes    : The 3' terminal A matches the G12D mutation (GGT > GAT).
           Perfect match at the 3' end allows polymerase extension from
           the mutant template; the A:G mismatch blocks extension from
           wild-type DNA, giving allele-specific amplification.

===========================================================================
2) Wild-type-specific forward primer  (KRAS_WT_Forward)
===========================================================================
Sequence : 5'- ACTTGTGGTAGTTGGAGCTGG -3'
Length   : 21 bp
GC%      : 52.4%
Tm       : ~59.5 °C  (estimated)
Notes    : Identical body to the mutant primer, but the 3' terminal G
           matches the wild-type codon 12 (GGT). Used as the allele-
           specificity control in the two-reaction AS-PCR scheme.

===========================================================================
3) Common reverse primer  (KRAS_Common_Reverse)
===========================================================================
Sequence : 5'- TGTATCGTCAAGGCACTCTTGC -3'
Length   : 22 bp
GC%      : 50.0%
Tm       : ~58.5 °C  (estimated)
Notes    : Binds downstream of codon 12 on the opposite strand and is
           shared by both the mutant and wild-type reactions. Finalise
           the exact binding site / amplicon length for your setup and
           validate with BLAST.

===========================================================================
Expected behaviour
===========================================================================
Mutant reaction    : amplification only when the G12D allele is present.
Wild-type reaction : amplification only when wild-type KRAS is present.
Common reaction    : confirms DNA quality and PCR functionality.
